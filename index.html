Bio:

Glass Animals are an English electro-indie band formed in 2010. The band originated in Oxford, UK where all four of the band members went to grade school together and became best friends. Glass Animals is led by lead singer, songwriter, and producer Dave Bayley. There is also Drew MacFarlane on guitar, keyboard, and backing vocals; Edmund Irwin-Singer on bass guitar, keyboards, and backing vocals; and Joe Seaward on drums and percussion.

Since the formation of the band, Glass Animals has gained international attention by creating a fanbase all over the world. With their truly unique style of music, it's no surprise that they have become so successful. To get a glimpse of their creativity and unique style, check out their offical website here.
